Our Approach

Built on top of state-of-the-art convolutional networks and advanced augmentation systems, our technology addresses key challenges in modern clinical imaging:

  • Enhanced dataset creation with synthetic image generation
  • Uncertainty scoring for reducing diagnostic ambiguity
  • Scalable CNN architecture optimized for robust multiclass prediction

Impact: Early identification prevents irreversible vision loss while reducing clinician workload through automated, trustworthy AI screening.
